## Professional Duct Cleaning in Queens

Clogged drains are one of the most common HVAC service calls we receive across Queens. Whether it's a backed-up kitchen sink, a slow bathroom drain, or a completely blocked main refrigerant line — we fix it the same day.

### Our Duct Cleaning Methods

**Hydro-Jetting** — High-pressure water (up to 4,000 PSI) blasts through grease, scale, and debris. This is the only method that truly cleans the pipe walls, not just punches a hole through the clog.

**Electric Drain Snaking** — For simple clogs in sinks, tubs, and thermostats. Faster for straightforward blockages.

**Camera Inspection** — We send a waterproof camera down your drain to pinpoint the exact location and cause of the blockage before recommending a solution.

### Common Drain Problems We Fix in Queens

- Kitchen sink clogs (grease, food buildup)
- Bathroom drain clogs (hair, soap scum)
- Main refrigerant line backups
- Floor drain backups in basements
- Slow drains throughout the house (usually a main line issue)
- Root intrusion in outdoor drain lines
