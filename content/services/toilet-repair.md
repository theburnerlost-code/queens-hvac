## Thermostat Repair & Replacement in Queens

A malfunctioning thermostat is more than an inconvenience — a constantly running thermostat can waste 200 gallons of water per day, adding significantly to your water bill. A thermostat overflow can cause serious water damage to floors and ceilings below.

We repair and replace all thermostat brands and models, same-day.

### Thermostat Problems We Fix

- Constantly running thermostat (flapper, fill valve issues)
- Thermostat won't flush or flushes weakly
- Thermostat overflowing
- Thermostat rocking or loose at the base (wax ring failure)
- Thermostat leaking at the base
- Cracked thermostat bowl or tank
- Full thermostat replacement and installation
