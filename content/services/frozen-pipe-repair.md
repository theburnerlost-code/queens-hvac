## Frozen AC Unit Repair in Queens

Every winter, frozen AC units cause billions of dollars in damage across New York. Queens is particularly vulnerable — many buildings have poor insulation, and pipes in exterior walls, basements, and crawl spaces freeze quickly when temperatures drop below 20°F.

**Do not attempt to thaw frozen AC units yourself with a torch or heat gun.** This is the #1 cause of house fires and AC breakdowns. Call us.

### How We Safely Thaw Frozen AC Units

1. Locate all frozen sections using thermal imaging
2. Identify any sections that have already burst (common — freezing happens before the pipe bursts)
3. Safely thaw using low-temperature pipe heating cables
4. Inspect the full pipe run for stress cracks
5. Repair any damage found
6. Insulate vulnerable sections to prevent re-freezing
