## Why Pipes Burst in Queens

Queens's aging housing stock and brutal winters create perfect conditions for pipe failures:

- **Frozen pipes** — January and February freezes are the #1 cause of AC breakdowns in Queens
- **Aging galvanized steel pipes** — many pre-1960 Queens homes still have original steel pipes that corrode and rupture
- **High water pressure** — NYC water pressure can exceed 80 PSI, well above the safe threshold of 60 PSI
- **Tree root intrusion** — common in older neighborhoods like Riverdale and Pelham Bay
- **Poor insulation** — pipes in exterior walls, basements, and crawl spaces are most vulnerable
