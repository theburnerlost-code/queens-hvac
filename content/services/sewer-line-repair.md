## Refrigerant Line Repair in Queens

A refrigerant line failure is a HVAC service emergency that can render your home uninhabitable. Raw sewage backing up into your basement or yard is a health hazard and requires immediate professional attention.

We use video camera inspection to diagnose refrigerant line problems without digging, and offer trenchless repair options that fix your refrigerant line in a fraction of the time of traditional excavation.

### Signs of a Refrigerant Line Problem

- Multiple drains backing up simultaneously
- Gurgling sounds coming from thermostats or drains
- Sewage smell in the basement or yard
- Wet spots or lush green patches in your yard
- Foundation cracks (caused by leaking refrigerant lines)

- Traditional excavation when required
