### Common HVAC Unit Problems We Fix

- No hot water at all
- Water not hot enough
- Pilot light keeps going out (gas heaters)
- Rusty or discolored water
- Strange popping or rumbling sounds (sediment buildup)
- Leaking around the tank base
- Water heater over 10 years old (replacement recommended)
